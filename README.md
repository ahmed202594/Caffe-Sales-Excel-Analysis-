### 2Ô∏è‚É£ Seasonal & Monthly Trends
*Sales are highly sensitive to weather and seasons.*

* **Peak Months:** March, October, February (Cool/Moderate weather).
* **Low Months:** July, January, April (Extreme weather or seasonal dips).
* **Insight:** Summer months (July) show a significant drop, suggesting a need for cold beverage promotions.

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Day & Hour Analysis
*The "Golden Hours" for sales are mid-morning and late afternoon.*

* **Busiest Days:** Tuesday & Monday (Strong start to the week).
* **Slowest Days:** Sunday (Weak weekend performance).
* **Peak Hours:** 10:00 AM & 4:00 PM.
* **Dead Hours:** Early Morning (6-7 AM) and Late Night (after 10 PM).

## üí° Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data analysis, the following actions are recommended to improve profitability:

1.  **Marketing Focus:**
    * Capitalize on **Milk-based drinks** (Latte/Cappuccino) by introducing new flavors (Vanilla, Hazelnut).
    * Launch **"Summer Cool Offers"** in July to counter the seasonal sales drop.

2.  **Operational Efficiency:**
    * Optimize staffing during **10 AM - 11 AM** and **4 PM** to handle peak traffic.
    * Consider reducing operating hours late at night (after 9 PM) or switching to delivery-only to save costs.

3.  **Pricing Strategy:**
    * Slight price increase (3-5%) on top-selling items (Latte) in peak months (March/Oct) is feasible.
    * Reposition **Espresso** as a premium product rather than discounting it.

4.  **Weekend Traffic:**
    * Create specific **Weekend Bundles** to boost low sales on Saturdays and Sundays.
